Honda has its share of performance bikes and the CBR150R adds to that portfolio. This one is the younger and more docile sibling of the popular CBR 250R. A host of design cues have been picked up from the elder one but it differentiates itself with the two-tone paint scheme Honda uses. The CBR150R is powered by a single cylinder, fuel-injected 149.4cc engine which produces 18.28bhp and it a fun thing to ride around the urban setting. It's mated to a 6-speed gearbox and Honda has managed to bring in a sense of sportiness and fuel efficiency as well.
